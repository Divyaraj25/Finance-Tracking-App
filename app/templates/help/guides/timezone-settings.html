Timezone Settings

Why Timezone Matters

Setting the correct timezone ensures that all dates and times in the system accurately reflect your local time. This is especially important for:

  • Transaction dates - When you actually made the purchase
  • Budget periods - Correct start and end of budget cycles
  • Reports - Accurate monthly and yearly summaries
  • Due dates - Credit card and bill payment reminders

Setting Your Timezone

1
Go to Profile Settings

Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Profile"

2
Navigate to General Settings

Click on the "General" tab in the settings section

3
Select Your Timezone

Find and select your timezone from the dropdown menu

"Local Time (Browser)" uses your computer's timezone settings
4
Save Changes

Click "Save General Settings" to apply your new timezone

Timezone Options

Option Description Use Case
Local Time (Browser) Uses your computer's timezone Best for most users, automatically adjusts
UTC Coordinated Universal Time For developers, international coordination
America/New_York US Eastern Time New York, Atlanta, Miami
Europe/London UK Time London, Dublin, Lisbon
Asia/Kolkata Indian Standard Time India, Sri Lanka

What Changes with Timezone

✅ Transaction Dates

All transaction dates will be converted to your local time

✅ Budget Periods

Budget start/end dates adjust to your timezone

✅ Due Dates

Payment reminders based on your local time

✅ Reports

Monthly reports aligned with your calendar

Troubleshooting

Solution: Refresh the page after saving settings. Some cached data may need to reload.
