Time Formats

12-Hour Format

3:45 PM

Common in US, Canada, Australia, Philippines

  • Uses AM/PM indicators
  • Hours from 1-12
  • More conversational
24-Hour Format

15:45

Common in Europe, military, scientific

  • No AM/PM ambiguity
  • Hours from 0-23
  • More precise for sorting

Week Start Options

Choose which day your week begins on. This affects:

  • Weekly budgets - When the budget period resets
  • Reports - Weekly summaries and charts
  • Calendar views - How the week is displayed
Option 1
Monday

International standard
Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun

Option 2
Sunday

US & Canada
Sun, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat

Option 3
Saturday

Middle East
Sat, Sun, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri

Changing Your Format

1
Go to Profile Settings

Click your profile icon and select "Profile"

2
Select General Tab

Navigate to the "General" settings tab

3
Choose Your Preferences

Select your preferred date format, time format, and week start

4
Save Changes

Click "Save General Settings" to apply
