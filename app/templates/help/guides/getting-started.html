Getting Started with Expense Tracker

Welcome to Expense Tracker! This guide will help you set up your account and start tracking your finances in less than 10 minutes.

1. Introduction

Expense Tracker is a comprehensive financial management system that helps you:

  • Track all your accounts in one place
  • Monitor your spending habits
  • Set and achieve budget goals
  • Generate financial reports
  • Export data for tax purposes
Pro Tip: The system is designed to be intuitive, but following this guide will help you avoid common mistakes.

2. Create Your First Account

Before adding transactions, you need to create at least one account:

1
Click "Create Account"

Navigate to Accounts page and click the "Add Account" button

2
Choose Account Type

Select from: Bank Account (checking/savings), Credit Card, Cash, Asset, or Liability

3
Enter Initial Balance

Start with your current account balance. You can adjust this later.

Important: Credit card accounts should have a negative balance to represent debt.

3. Add Your First Transaction

1
Click "Add Transaction"

Found on both Dashboard and Transactions pages

2
Select Transaction Type
  • Income - Money received (salary, gifts)
  • Expense - Money spent (groceries, bills)
  • Transfer - Move money between accounts
3
Fill in the Details

Enter amount, description, category, and accounts

Field Example
Amount $50.00
Description Grocery shopping at Walmart
Category Food & Dining
Account Checking Account
4
Save Transaction

Click "Save" and watch your account balance update instantly

4. Understanding the Dashboard

Summary Cards

At-a-glance view of:

  • Total balance across all accounts
  • Monthly income & expenses
  • Budget status
Charts

Visual representation of:

  • Income vs Expense trends
  • Spending by category
  • Account distribution
Recent Transactions

Your latest 5 transactions with:

  • Date and description
  • Amount and category
  • Account name
Quick Actions

Buttons to:

  • Add new transaction
  • Refresh data
  • Apply filters

5. Setting Up Budgets

Why budget? Budgets help you control spending and save money.
1
Go to Budgets Page

Click "Budgets" in the main navigation

2
Create a Budget

Click "Create Budget" and select:

  • Category - What you want to track
  • Amount - Your spending limit
  • Period - Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly
3
Monitor Progress

Each budget shows:

  • Spent amount and remaining
  • Progress bar with color coding
  • Warning when nearing limit
4
Adjust as Needed

Edit budget amount or period anytime. Recalculate if transactions were added before budget creation.
